# EngiBot 🤖  

EngiBot is an **AI-powered tutoring assistant** designed to help engineering students learn more effectively.  
It provides **on-demand answers**, recommends **relevant learning materials**, and guides students through problem-solving steps in a conversational way.  

The system is powered by a **Rasa-based chatbot** with a custom knowledge retrieval pipeline that searches through course materials (PDFs, slides, notes) to provide contextual and accurate responses.  
Each student interaction is logged (securely) to personalize future assistance and track learning progress.

### ✨ Features
- **Conversational Q&A** – ask questions about your course material in plain language.  
- **Context-Aware Search** – retrieves the most relevant pages from the PDFs you’ve studied.  
- **Personalized Assistance** – adapts to your profile and tracks your progress over time.  
- **Web Interface** – no installation needed, just click a link and start chatting.  

---

### 📌 How to Use
1. Click the link for your course above.  
2. Log in with your university account (if required).  
3. Ask EngiBot a question – try “Explain X in simple terms” or “Show me examples of Y”.  
4. Explore recommended materials or follow-up questions suggested by the bot.  

---

### 💡 Why Use EngiBot?
EngiBot is built to make studying more **interactive**, **efficient**, and **engaging**.  
Instead of searching through slides or notes manually, you can have a conversation with the bot and focus on what really matters: **understanding the concepts**.
